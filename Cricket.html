Cricket


The game of cricket started in England first, but now it is played all over the globe and is very popular. Cricket is a bat-and-ball game that is played between two teams with 11 players in each of them. The field is oval in shape with a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch in the center. In the game process, one team bats and tries to score runs, the more, the better, while the second team fields and bowls, and attempts to dismiss the batsmen and limit runs received by the first team. Teams switch between batting and fielding at the end of innings.

The game's governing body is the International Cricket Council (ICC), which has over 100 members, twelve of which are full members who play Test matches. Cricket is a multi-faceted sport with multiple formats that can effectively be divided into first-class cricket, limited overs cricket and, historically, single wicket cricket.
